Animated GIF

Images - Description

The animated GIF will be used in social campaign to show the process of landscaping design. The animated GIF is based on the EPS image, bought from Shutterstock and processed in Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop. The black and white objects in the EPS file were rearranged, coloured and reduced/enlarged without loss in different steps in AI. At every step a file was converted to a raster image and later included as a layer/frame in Photoshop. The output file (376x254, 1.79MB ) was chosen to meet the following requirements of Facebook and Twitter for Animated GIFs: Minimum to appear expanded 440 x 220 pixels; Maximum to appear expanded 1024 x 512 pixels; Appears in stream collapsed at 506 x 253 pixels on desktop; Maximum file size of 5 MB for animated GIFs on mobile and 15 MB on web.
